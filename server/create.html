PROMPT WORLD

Worlds made of prompts. You describe a stage — your AI builds it — anyone plays it from a URL.

STEP 1 — CONNECT YOUR CLAUDE

On claude.ai: Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector, and paste:

https://promptworld.pages.dev/mcp

Using Claude Code instead? Run:

claude mcp add --transport http promptworld https://promptworld.pages.dev/mcp

STEP 2 — SAY WHAT YOU WANT

Just describe a stage. For example:

Create a Prompt World stage: a gravity-flip corridor
where I run on the ceiling to dodge spikes, then a big
boost launch into the goal. Time limit 45 seconds.

Your Claude reads the toolbox, designs the level, and hands you a test link.

STEP 3 — PROVE IT, THEN PUBLISH

The one rule: a stage can only be published after you clear it in the browser. Your run is recorded and mathematically verified by the server — impossible stages cannot exist here.

Clear your test link, tell your Claude to publish, and share the play URL anywhere. Friends tap it and play instantly — no install, no account, free.

LIMITS

Time limit 5s–30min · up to 300 parts per stage · 5 publishes per day. Stages that take under 3 seconds are too trivial to publish.
