### Description (Play 4000 chars / iOS 4000 chars)
Prompt World: TAC is a minimalist 3D tactical stealth shooter you can pick up in
seconds.

Move quietly, watch enemy sightlines, and pick your moment. Get spotted and the
whole patrol converges — soldiers give chase, gatling emplacements pin you down,
snipers paint you with a laser before the kill shot, and scout drones dive from
above. Break line of sight, flank, and use the terrain to survive.

FEATURES
• Pure, readable design — every threat is something you can see and outplay.
• A toolbox of enemies: patrolling soldiers, gatling nests, snipers, kamikaze
  drones, shield bearers, and armored vehicles that only crack from the rear.
• Capture an enemy drone and pilot it for a one-shot strike.
• Scoped sniping, grenades, and stealth takedowns.
• Endless variety: play stages built by other players, and build your own.

Free to play, quick to learn, tense to master. How quietly can you clear it?

静かに動き、敵の視線を読み、好機を待て。見つかれば巡回兵が一斉に集まる——兵士は

## Store checklist reminders (not copy — for you)
- Screenshots: iOS needs 6.7" and 6.5"; Play needs at least 2 (phone). Capture
  from a real run — a tense spotted moment reads well.
- iOS privacy "data collection": declare IDFA + usage data collected for ads.
- Play "Data safety": same — advertising ID + app activity, for advertising.
- Both: set "contains ads" and answer the age-rating questionnaire.
- Privacy policy URL: https://<your-domain>/privacy (privacy.html, now AdMob-aware).
- Play also needs a 512px icon and a 1024×500 feature graphic.
